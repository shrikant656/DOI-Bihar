Single Window Clearance System

SWCS for industries is a concept to provide all entry level services to entrepreneurs,

with minimum interface with departments, in a transparent manner, within specified time leading to hassle free approvals. There is a provision for deemed approvals for certain clearances. Under the Single Window Act, Government have notified and constituted different Agencies /Committees for receiving, processing and monitoring of applications under Single Window Act.
